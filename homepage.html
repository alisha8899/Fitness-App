About

Lyfestyle has been created by Albert Ong, Gary Chang, Alisha Mehndiratta, and Alexis Arroyo. We believe that it is important to track your food consumption, in order to achieve any goals for your fitness and health. This platform will allow you to track your food, water, exercise, along with helpful tips through your journey.
